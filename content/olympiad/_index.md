This section contains a selection of math problems from olympiads, books, and other contests that I have found interesting. I keep them here in the hope that they will be useful to others who enjoy solving competitive math problems, as well as to students looking for training material.

It is a living collection: as I discover, solve, or compose new problems, I will continue to add them here. You can follow updates through this [RSS feed](/rss/olympiad.xml).

Disclaimer:
- The tagging and the difficulty system needs to be improved, for example, some `easy` problems are tagged as `medium` and vice-versa;
- The list contains mostly problems of `medium` difficulty, at the level of regional romanian math olympiads (etapa judeteana), or easier. In time I plan to add more difficult problems.
- Most of the problems were extracted from previous articles on the blog, so there might be formatting issues, etc.
- If you find errors, please report them or open a PR directly.
