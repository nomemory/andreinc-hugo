<p><a class="mpl" href="/olympiad/p00065/">Problem 00065</a></p> 
<p>Let \(x_1, x_2, \dots x_n \in \mathbb{R}_{+}\), prove:</p>
<p class="mpc">
\[\sum_{i=1}^n \left[\frac{1}{1+\sum_{j=1}^i x_j}\right] \lt \sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^n \frac{1}{x_i}} \]
</p>
