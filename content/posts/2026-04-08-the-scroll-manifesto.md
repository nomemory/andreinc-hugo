How to use this marvelous tool:

1. Bookmark this page.
2. Whenever you feel the urge to open your favorite social media app, open this link instead.
3. Scroll for a few moments.
4. Remember you are human.
5. Stop scrolling like this. 
6. Scroll in a document.

<small>You don't need to all that. Just number five.</small>

---

What was achieved here? 

It is proof that the modern wall does not need meaning, only rhythm. Have you noticed a weird cadence in the way you scroll? 

> A nice face moves its lips, the eyes blink, the hand scrolls, Pinocchio's nose oscillates.

It does not matter if the content is politics, celebrity, science, career advice, or a cute cat painted like [The Aristocats](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Aristocats) (before *the skibidi era* children loved those). 

This `<<anything>>` if placed inside the machine, it becomes fuel. The *machine* is almost admirable as it works with `<<anything>>` fuel, and lately with all the *reels* and the *shorts* the `<<anything>>` fuel seems to become more potent. It now accepts `<<bio>>` fuel, and `<<ai>>` fuel and `<<hybrid>>` fuel, they all seem to burn ~~you~~ the same.

Yes, the *feed* is ridiculous, cringe, vulgar, repetitive, sloppy (AI), needy, but at least it is honest about the chemical reactions it wants to provoke in our brains. Maybe this is its only virtue: it no longer hides what it wants. It wants our time, our thumb, and our nerves.

So the point is not moral panic, although we should panic a little. The point is clarity. Look at the wall and call it *a wall* (no, not that [wall](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LB44aM_9IQA&list=PL0n75a7HekRLihb49yWOUua58pMbMCvjm&index=26)). Look at the hook and call it [*a hook*](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Captain_Hook), and eat his hand like a crocodile. Maybe, laugh a little if you wish (I remember there was a column with jokes in the old newspapers).

But turn it OFF before the mechanism begins to speak in your own ~~inner~~ voice. There is such a thing as too late for that, and we ~~will~~ might soon find out.
